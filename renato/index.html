About

Professor Renato Spigler is a Senior Professor at Roma Tre University, with an extensive career spanning over five decades in mathematical analysis, numerical analysis, and mathematical physics. Born in Venice, Italy, on November 10, 1947, he has made significant contributions to applied mathematics, numerical analysis, and computational physics.

Education

  • Laurea in Electronic Engineering, University of Padova (1972)
  • Specialization in Computing Machines, University of Bologna (1974)

Current Position

  • Senior Professor, Roma Tre University (2018-present) and ''Extraordinary Professor'' at Uninettuno Telematic International University
  • Previously Full Professor of Numerical Analysis (2013-2018)
  • Full Professor of Mathematical Analysis (1997-2013)

International Experience

  • University of Wisconsin-Madison
  • New York Universuity (NYU), Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences

My name is Stefano Spigler. I am a postdoc in the Laboratory of Physics of Complex Systems at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland). My current research focuses on the foundations of modern deep learning algorithms. In my most recent works I have studied (i) how over-parametrization affects the loss landscape and the learning dynamics; (ii) how the generalization error of kernel methods scales with the size of the training set; (iii) the limit of large hidden layers in deep networks.

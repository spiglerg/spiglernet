Giacomo Spigler

I am a (tenure-track) Assistant Professor at the Department of Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence of Tilburg University, where I work on various topics in Deep Learning and Deep Reinforcement Learning.
I am interested in artificial (general) intelligence and in building intelligent artificial life. In particular, I am interested in continual learning, meta-learning, and innate evolutionary priors.

Giacomo Spigler

I am Assistant Professor at the Department of Intelligent Systems of Tilburg University, where I work on various topics in AI for Robotics, especially Deep Reinforcement Learning and Imitation Learning.
I am interested in artificial (general) intelligence and in building intelligent artificial life. In particular, I am interested in deep reinforcement learning for robotics, meta-learning, and innate evolutionary priors.
